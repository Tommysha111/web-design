About Us

Our History

Founded in 2020, Sierra Leone Football Agency emerged from a passionate vision to transform the landscape of football talent management in Sierra Leone. Our journey began with a small team of dedicated professionals who recognized the untapped potential of local football talent.

Over the past five years, we have grown from representing a handful of players to becoming one of the most trusted football agencies in the region. We have successfully facilitated numerous transfers to national and international clubs, helping our players achieve their dreams while putting Sierra Leone football on the global map.

Today, we stand proud as a beacon of excellence in football representation, continuing to discover and nurture the next generation of football stars from Sierra Leone.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become the leading football agency in West Africa, recognized for our commitment to developing world-class talent and providing unparalleled opportunities for Sierra Leonean footballers on the international stage.

We envision a future where Sierra Leone is known globally as a powerhouse of football talent, with our players competing at the highest levels of professional football across Europe, Africa, and beyond. Through strategic partnerships, cutting-edge training programs, and unwavering dedication to our players' success, we aim to revolutionize how African football talent is discovered, developed, and promoted worldwide.

We believe that every talented young player in Sierra Leone deserves the opportunity to showcase their skills and achieve their full potential, regardless of their background or circumstances.

Our Mission

Our mission is to identify, develop, and represent exceptional football talent from Sierra Leone, providing comprehensive support services that enable our players to reach their maximum potential both on and off the field.

We are committed to maintaining the highest standards of professionalism, integrity, and transparency in all our dealings. We work tirelessly to secure the best opportunities for our players while ensuring their welfare, education, and personal development remain at the forefront of everything we do.

Through strategic partnerships with clubs, scouts, and football organizations worldwide, we create pathways for our players to compete at national and international levels. We provide expert guidance on contract negotiations, career planning, and professional development to ensure our players make informed decisions about their football careers.
